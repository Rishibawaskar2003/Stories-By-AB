Company Introduction

At Stories by AB, we believe that every wedding is not just an event, but a beautiful story waiting to be told. Based in Pune, we are a team of passionate photographers and filmmakers who specialize in capturing the essence of love, emotions, traditions, and timeless celebrations. With over seven years of experience in the wedding industry, we have had the privilege of documenting countless love stories, each unique in its own way.

Our approach goes beyond simply taking photos or recording videos. We focus on storytelling—on capturing real emotions, unscripted moments, and the little details that often go unnoticed but mean the most. From the nervous smiles before the ceremony to the joyful tears, laughter, and vibrant celebrations, we ensure that every frame reflects the authenticity and beauty of your special day.

At Stories by AB, we combine creativity with technical expertise to deliver visuals that are not only aesthetically stunning but also emotionally powerful. Whether it’s cinematic wedding films, candid photography, or traditional coverage, our goal is to create a seamless blend of art and reality. We pay close attention to lighting, composition, and storytelling techniques to craft memories that feel alive every time you revisit them.

We understand that weddings are once-in-a-lifetime moments, and preserving them perfectly is a responsibility we take seriously. Our team works closely with every couple to understand their vision, preferences, and personality, ensuring that the final output truly represents who they are. Every wedding we capture is treated with dedication, precision, and a commitment to excellence.

Over the years, our work has been driven by one simple philosophy—to create timeless memories that you can cherish forever. We don’t just deliver photos and videos; we deliver emotions, experiences, and stories that stay with you for a lifetime.

Our Services

Traditional Videography

A traditional videographer captures your wedding in a continuous documentary style format covering every ritual event and moment as it happens just like how you'd watch it on a home video.

Traditional Photography

A traditional photographer focuses on capturing posed structured and formal shorts of the wedding day their style ensures that all important rituals family members and group photes are documented clearly and completely.

Cinematography

The art of capturing motion through visuals. It combines lighting, camera angles, framing, and movement to tell powerful stories on screen, blending creativity with technical skill.

Candid Photography

Candid Photography captures genuine, unposed moments as they naturally unfold. It focuses on real emotions, spontaneity, and storytelling without staged setups.
